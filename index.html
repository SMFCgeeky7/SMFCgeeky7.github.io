Majadito de Charque

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Egg *As you want
Charque ½ kg
Rice 1 kg
Onion 1
Pepper ½
Garlic 2 cloves minced
Sybarite 3 spoons
Cumin *As you want
Plantains *As you want
Yuca *As you want
Salt & Oil *As you want

Step 1: Overcook the rice with urucu oil and once it starts getting a obtaining a gold-colored appereace add the onion, pepper, garlic and finally the charque.

Step 2: As soon as it is cooked, add cups of broth and a pinch of cumin. Let simmer until it decreases or the broth dries.

Step 3: Serve with plantains and fried eggs. If you want you can add some tomato and onion salad.

Locro de Gallina

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Creole hen 1
Rice 2 cups
Water 3 litters
"Imilla" potato 3
Oil ¼ cup
Onions *As you want
Peppers *As you want
Salt *As you want
Eggs *As you want

Step 1: Lay in a boiling pot the following: water, the cut up chicken, and fry with a bit of oil the onions and sliced peppers.

Step 2: The moment that the chicken pieces are gold-colored, place them in other pot with water and start boiling. Beware if the pieces fall apart.

Step 3: After 25 minutes, add rice, sliced potatos, colorant and salt. Be vigilant for the rice and crack the eggs in the same pot.

Picante de Gallina

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken pieces 8
Onion 3
Tomato 2
Pea ¾ cup
Peruvian red pepper ¾ cup
Garlic *As you want
Cumin *As you want
Pepper *As you want
Salt *As you want

Step 1: In a mixer or blender with little water, grind the seasonings, until it has a consistency of puree. Reserve.

Step 2: Cut up and wash the chicken well, removing its viscera.

Step 3: In a pot with ¼ cup of oil, fry the onion until all its juice comes out and it is colored then add the ground chili, stirring until it takes a dark tone, add the tomato, stirring another 5 min to 10 min.

Step 4: Add 5 cups of boiled water or broth and boil for 15 to 20 minutes.

Step 5: After 20 minutes of cooking the drowning, add the quartered chicken pieces, if you can open the pieces to allow the flavor of the chili to pass, check the salt and add any seasoning of taste. Let it cook for another 20 minutes.

Step 6: In another pot place water and when it is boiling add the peas and leave them for about 10 minutes because they have to be pre-cooked and super green, serve the preparation with a whole potato, chuño puthi, grained rice, and tomato salad with onion ( zarsa) and add the peas on top.

Rapi Al Jugo

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Rapi 1 Kg
Red colorant *As you want
Garlic cloves 3
Carrots (Optional) 3
Green sliced peppers 2
Sliced Onions 3
Sliced tomatos 8 (or required quantity)
Garlic *As you want
Black pepper *As you want
Cumin *As you want
Salt *As you want
Oil *As you want

Step 1: Season the rapi meat with the condiments

Step 2: Seal the meat in oil.

Step 3: Add all the other vegetables and stir while adding some color with the colorant.

Step 4: Taste the flavors and place the meat onto this preparation. Once again taste the flavor and make sure it has a pleasant taste. In the other hand, add a few aromatic herbs.

Step 5: Cover it up and let cook for at least 2 hours.

Step 6: If neccesary, add hot water.

Step 7: Move away once the meat is cooked and hot.

Garnish: Rapi must go with rice, toasted or with cheese, cooked yuca and tomato and onion salad. It's way more tasty with green onions.
